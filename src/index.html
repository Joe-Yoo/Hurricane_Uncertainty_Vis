Hurricane Uncertainty Visualization

Exploring forecast track uncertainty through interactive spaghetti models

Hurricane track forecasts are inherently uncertain. This study presents three separate visualizations — a cone of uncertainty, a spaghetti model, and an interactive ensemble view — each representing a different approach to communicating that uncertainty to the public and decision-makers.

These visualizations are independent of one another and are intended to be compared. As you explore each one, consider how effectively it conveys the range of possible storm paths and what conditions a given location might face.

Please open the survey link below and fill it out as you go through each visualization. Your responses will help us understand which design best supports understanding of hurricane forecast uncertainty.
