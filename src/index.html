Hurricane Uncertainty Visualization

Exploring forecast track uncertainty through interactive spaghetti models

Hurricane track forecasts are inherently uncertain. Meteorologists generate ensembles of possible paths, often called spaghetti models, to communicate this uncertainty to the public and decision-makers.

This tool lets you interact with those ensemble tracks. You can select individual lines of interest or define a geographic radius to highlight tracks that pass near a location you care about.

Use the left map to explore tracks spatially. Use the right map to inspect meteorological attributes (wind speed, temperature, and precipitation) for the selected tracks.
